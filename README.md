## Research Directions

**Battery interfaces and diagnostics**  
Battery data should remain physically meaningful: cycling behavior, degradation signals, electrolyte/interface changes, and reporting details that make experiments comparable.

**ALD and thin-film processing**  
My earlier work on ALD-coated Zn metal anodes made me interested in process-property questions: when a coating or film changes interfacial chemistry, electrochemical stability, or device behavior in a measurable way.

**Materials informatics**  
I am cautious about generic "AI for materials" claims. The work I want to do is closer to data quality, uncertainty, literature-derived datasets, and models that stay tied to physical interpretation.
