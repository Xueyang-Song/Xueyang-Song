## Research Directions

### Battery Interfaces and Diagnostics

I am interested in battery data that remains physically meaningful: cycling behavior, degradation signals, electrolyte/interface changes, and the reporting details needed for useful comparison across experiments.

### ALD and Thin-Film Processing

My earlier work on ALD-coated Zn metal anodes made me interested in process-property questions: when a coating or thin film changes interfacial chemistry, electrochemical stability, or device behavior in a measurable way.

### Materials Informatics

I am cautious about generic "AI for materials" claims. The work I want to do is closer to data quality, uncertainty, literature-derived datasets, and models that stay tied to physical interpretation.

## Background

Before software engineering, I worked on electrochemical materials research, including Zn-ion batteries, ALD surface engineering, aqueous electrolytes, and synchrotron XAFS-supported interface analysis.
